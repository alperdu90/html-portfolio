My Best Movies

My top 3 movie franchises of all-time

The Matrix

The original trilogy is amazing. The 4th film doesn't fit in the trilogy but I like it regardless. There is also Animatrix which is a series of short animated films from the Matrix universe. That is also very good.

The Lord of the Rings

The Lotr has three movies and they are all amazing. There are also extended versions that deserve a rewatch. I think we can also put the Hobbit trilogy into the Lotr franchise. I like the Hobbit movies as well.

The Godfather

It has three movies. I like them all. A true classic.
